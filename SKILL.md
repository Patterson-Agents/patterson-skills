## Quick reference
- **Brand:** Patterson Companies, Inc. — "Trusted Expertise. Unrivaled Support." Since 1877.
- **Colors:** Navy `#003767`, Sky `#00A8E1`, cool grays. Secondary blues; tertiary green/teal/purple for data only.
- **Type:** Proxima Nova (real Patterson font, Adobe Fonts — add your Typekit embed; Figtree is the loaded free fallback). Bold navy headlines, cool-gray body, big sky stats.
- **Shape:** Pill buttons (navy→sky on hover), 10px cards, soft navy-tinted shadows, sky focus ring. Clean, calm, corporate. No emoji.
- **Tokens:** link `styles.css`. **Components:** `window.PattersonCompaniesDesignSystem_1f7cbe`.
- **Files:** `readme.md` (full guide), `tokens/`, `components/`, `guidelines/` (specimens), `ui_kits/corporate-website/`, `ui_kits/storefront/` (Dental + Vet), `templates/corporate-page/`, `assets/` (incl. `brand/patterson-mark.svg`).
